<h2 class="topic-title">The Cricketing Sensation: Virat Kohli</h2>

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who has taken the world by storm with his exceptional batting skills. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has become a household name in the cricketing fraternity.

Kohli's passion for cricket began at a young age, and he started playing cricket at the tender age of nine. His hard work and dedication earned him a place in the Delhi Under-15 team.

In 2008, Kohli led the Indian Under-19 team to victory in the World Cup, held in Malaysia. This achievement marked the beginning of his illustrious career.

Kohli made his international debut in 2008 against Sri Lanka. His impressive performance in the series earned him a permanent place in the Indian team.

In 2011, Kohli played a crucial role in India's World Cup victory, scoring 282 runs in the tournament. His aggressive batting style and exceptional fielding skills made him a fan favorite.

Kohli has been awarded numerous accolades, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2012 and 2017. He has also been named the captain of the Indian Test team.
